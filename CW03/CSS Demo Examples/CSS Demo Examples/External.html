A chilling tale

Edgar Allan Poe was a noted author of scary stories. This is not one of them. It has no purpose other than to illustrate some styles. Here is what an anonymous author had to say:

It was a dark and stormy night. The officer of Napoleon's army rode furiously through the lashing rain. As he approached the hilltop, he could see a light dimly shining through the gloom.

Amazing, isn't it?
